INCIDENT REPORT
Report ID: IR-2026-003
Date: 10 March 2026
Operator: R. Kumar
Asset ID: AST-0042
Equipment: Pump P-102 - Motor
Plant: Plant A
Location: Section 3
Failure Type: Motor overheating

DESCRIPTION
At 11:00 hours, motor temperature alarm triggered for Pump P-102 motor. Winding temperature measured at 145°C (normal operating range: 70-90°C). Pump was tripped automatically by the thermal overload protection.

ROOT CAUSE
Blocked discharge line caused pump to operate at near-zero flow condition, resulting in rapid heat buildup in the motor. The blockage was traced to debris (scale deposits) in the discharge pipe following recent pipeline maintenance work.

CORRECTIVE ACTION
1. Isolated pump and locked out power supply.
2. Removed debris from discharge line (scale buildup approximately 2 kg).
3. Inspected motor windings - no visible damage, megger test passed at 200 MΩ.
4. Serviced motor bearings and applied grease.
5. Restarted pump at 17:00 hours - temperature stabilized at 82°C.

DOWNTIME: 6 hours

RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Install Y-strainer on pump discharge line.
2. Add post-maintenance flush procedure to pipeline maintenance SOPs.
3. Monitor motor temperature closely for next 30 days.
