INCIDENT REPORT
Report ID: IR-2026-004
Date: 05 April 2026
Operator: A. Singh
Asset ID: AST-0055
Equipment: Cooling Fan CF-101
Plant: Plant B
Location: Roof
Failure Type: Fan blade fracture

DESCRIPTION
At approximately 03:00 hours, a loud bang was heard from the roof area. Cooling Fan CF-101 (AST-0055) was found to have one of its six fan blades completely fractured at the root. The fan was vibrating severely and was immediately stopped.

ROOT CAUSE
Material fatigue after 18 months of continuous operation. Metallurgical analysis of the fractured blade showed crack initiation at a weld point on the blade root. The fan had been running 24/7 since installation in October 2024.

CORRECTIVE ACTION
1. Replaced entire fan assembly with upgraded unit (ABB AC-500-Series 2).
2. Installed vibration monitoring sensor on the new fan.
3. Verified balance of new fan assembly.
4. Updated maintenance schedule to include quarterly blade inspections.

DOWNTIME: 3 hours

RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Install vibration monitoring on all roof fans.
2. Replace fan assemblies after 15,000 operating hours as proactive measure.
3. Consider intermittent fan operation to reduce continuous stress.
