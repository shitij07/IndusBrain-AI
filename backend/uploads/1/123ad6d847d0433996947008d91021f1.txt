INCIDENT REPORT
Report ID: IR-2026-007
Date: 2026-06-15
Operator: R. Kumar
Asset ID: AST-0042
Equipment: Pump P-102
Plant: Plant A
Location: Section 3
Failure Type: Seal leakage

DESCRIPTION
Operator R. Kumar detected fluid leakage from Pump P-102 seal housing for the third time this year. Leakage rate approximately 20 drops/min. Seal was replaced just 5 months ago during the January incident.

ROOT CAUSE
Shaft sleeve has developed a groove from the previous seal running surface, causing premature seal wear. The shaft sleeve was not inspected/replaced during the January 2026 seal replacement.

CORRECTIVE ACTION
1. Replaced shaft sleeve.
2. Installed new mechanical seal.
3. Verified shaft runout within spec (0.03 mm max).
4. Pump restarted successfully.

RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Always inspect shaft sleeve when replacing mechanical seals on Pump P-102.
2. Add shaft sleeve inspection to SOP-4205 seal replacement procedure.
