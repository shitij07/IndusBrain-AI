INCIDENT REPORT
Report ID: IR-2026-002
Date: February 20, 2026
Operator: S. Patel
Asset ID: AST-0043
Equipment: Compressor C-201
Plant: Plant A
Location: Compressor Bay
Failure Type: Bearing failure

DESCRIPTION
At 09:15 hours, operator S. Patel reported unusual grinding noise from Compressor C-201 (AST-0043). Vibration readings showed elevated levels at the drive-end bearing (12.4 mm/s vs normal 3.0 mm/s). Unit was shut down immediately.

ROOT CAUSE
Lubrication failure due to clogged oil feed line. The oil analysis showed metal particles indicating bearing raceway degradation.

CORRECTIVE ACTION
1. Replaced both drive-end and non-drive-end bearings (SKF 6310).
2. Flushed oil system and replaced oil filter.
3. Verified oil feed line is clear.
4. Restarted compressor at 17:30 hours - vibration readings at 2.8 mm/s (normal).

DOWNTIME: 8 hours

RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Implement monthly oil analysis for Compressor C-201.
2. Replace oil filter every 3 months instead of current 6-month schedule.
