INCIDENT REPORT
Report ID: IR-2026-001-DUP
Date: 15 January 2026
Operator: R. Kumar
Asset ID: AST-0042
Equipment: Pump P-102
Plant: Plant A
Location: Section 3
Failure Type: Seal leakage

DESCRIPTION
At approximately 14:30 hours, operator R. Kumar noticed excessive fluid leakage from the seal housing of Pump P-102 (AST-0042). The leakage rate was estimated at 30-40 drops per minute, significantly exceeding the acceptable weepage rate of 1-2 drops/min.

The pump was immediately shut down following SOP-4205 Section 5.0.

ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
Upon inspection, the mechanical seal was found to be worn. The seal ring had developed a groove on the stationary face, likely due to normal wear after approximately 18 months of continuous operation.

CORRECTIVE ACTION
1. Pump P-102 was isolated and lockout/tagout applied.
2. Mechanical seal replaced with genuine Grundfos seal kit (Part No: CR-45-3-SEAL).
3. Shaft inspected for scoring - no damage found.
4. Seal housing cleaned and reassembled.
5. Pump restarted at 18:30 hours - leakage rate verified at 1 drop/min (acceptable).

DOWNTIME: 4 hours

RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Schedule seal replacement every 12 months as proactive maintenance.
2. Update SOP-4205 to include monthly seal weepage inspection.
