INCIDENT REPORT
Report ID: IR-2026-005
Date: 12 May 2026
Operator: P. Verma
Asset ID: AST-0099
Equipment: Conveyor Belt CB-001
Plant: Plant B
Location: Bay 2
Failure Type: Belt misalignment

DESCRIPTION
At 08:30 hours, conveyor belt CB-001 (AST-0099) was found tracking to the left by approximately 50 mm. The belt was rubbing against the support frame, causing edge damage. Production line was stopped.

ROOT CAUSE
Worn idler pulley bearing on the return side allowed the pulley to tilt, causing the belt to drift.

CORRECTIVE ACTION
1. Replaced worn idler pulley bearing (SKF 6205).
2. Realigned belt tracking using adjustment bolts on the tail pulley.
3. Verified tracking by running belt empty for 30 minutes.
4. Inspected all other idler pulleys - one additional bearing showed early wear and was replaced proactively.

DOWNTIME: 5 hours

RECOMMENDATIONS
1. Implement monthly idler pulley inspection for all conveyors.
2. Add belt tracking check to daily startup checklist.
3. Replace all idler bearings at 5-year intervals.
