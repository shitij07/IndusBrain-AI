STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
SOP Number: SOP-4205
Title: Pump P-102 Startup and Shutdown
Plant: Plant A
Location: Section 3
Asset ID: AST-0042
Equipment: Pump P-102
Manufacturer: Grundfos
Model: CR-45-3
Category: Pump
Criticality: High
Status: Active

1.0 PURPOSE
This procedure describes the safe startup and shutdown of Pump P-102.

2.0 SCOPE
Applies to all operators working with Pump P-102 (AST-0042) in Plant A, Section 3.

3.0 PREREQUISITES
3.1 Verify pump isolation valve is closed.
3.2 Ensure suction line is fully primed.
3.3 Check lubrication oil level in bearing housing.
3.4 Verify coupling alignment between motor and pump shaft.
3.5 Confirm all pressure gauges (PT-402) are reading within normal range.

4.0 STARTUP PROCEDURE
4.1 Open suction valve fully.
4.2 Start the motor and immediately check for unusual vibrations or noise.
4.3 Gradually open the discharge valve while monitoring discharge pressure.
4.4 Verify flow rate is within 45-55 m³/h.
4.5 Check seal housing for any leakage. Slight weepage (1-2 drops/min) is acceptable.
4.6 Log all startup parameters in the maintenance log.

5.0 SHUTDOWN PROCEDURE
5.1 Close discharge valve gradually.
5.2 Stop the motor.
5.3 Close suction valve.
5.4 If shutting down for maintenance, isolate and lockout/tagout the pump.
5.5 Depressurize the system by cracking the drain valve.
