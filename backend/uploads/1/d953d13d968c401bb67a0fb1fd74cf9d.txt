1.0 GENERAL DESCRIPTION
The Grundfos CR-45-3 is a vertical multistage centrifugal pump designed for high-pressure water and light industrial fluid applications.

2.0 TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
- Flow Rate: 45 m³/h at 50 Hz
- Max Operating Pressure: 25 bar
- Motor Power: 15 kW
- Motor Speed: 2900 RPM
- Inlet/Outlet: DN80 / DN65
- Materials: Stainless steel impellers and chamber
- Weight: 185 kg

3.0 INSTALLATION
3.1 Foundation must be level within 0.5 mm/m.
3.2 Allow minimum 500 mm clearance on all sides for maintenance access.
3.3 Install isolation valves on both suction and discharge sides.
3.4 Ensure piping is supported independently to avoid stress on pump nozzles.
3.5 Connect pressure gauge at discharge (range: 0-30 bar).

4.0 OPERATION
4.1 Do not run pump dry.
4.2 Minimum flow rate: 5 m³/h.
4.3 Maximum ambient temperature: 50°C.
4.4 Maximum fluid temperature: 120°C.
4.5 Run pump at lowest possible NPSH margin.

5.0 MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE
- Daily: Check for leaks, unusual noise, vibration
- Weekly: Verify pressure and flow readings, log readings
- Monthly: Inspect seal weepage, check oil level
- 6-Month: Change bearing oil, inspect coupling
- 12-Month: Replace mechanical seal, inspect bearings, meg test motor
- 5-Year: Major overhaul - replace all bearings and seals

6.0 TROUBLESHOOTING
- Low Flow: Check suction strainer, verify valve position
- High Vibration: Check coupling alignment, bearing condition
- Seal Leakage: Replace mechanical seal, check shaft runout
- Motor Overheating: Check flow rate, verify cooling fan, clean motor fins
